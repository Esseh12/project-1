Trade Finance

Leverage on our teams and bespoke solutions to help you trade with confidence in both domestic & international markets.

Download Trade Finance Forms

Discover new markets with our Global Trade Portal



Access Global Trade Portal

Tailor-made solutions for buyers, sellers and traders

Our experienced team work alongside you to understand and support your business by providing trade finance solutions and services to facilitate your domestic and/or international trading activities.

Buyers

As a buyer, your main concerns are:

  • Import solutions
  • Secure your deal
  • Manage your cash flow



Our solutions

Import Letter of Credit

Import your goods on your terms while assuring payments to your suppliers upon presentation of complying documents which are in accordance with UCP 600 and the letter of credit.

Documentary Collection

Proceed with your payments upon proof of shipment from your supplier while you import with peace of mind.

Bank Guarantee / Standby Letters of Credit

Secure your deal with your beneficiary by leveraging MCBs reputation and assurance of payment on your behalf upon the presentation of complying documents by the supplier.

Import Loan

Trade with confidence and benefit from the experience and support of our specialists.

Traders

As a trader, your main concerns are:

  • Secure your deal
  • Manage your cash flow



Our solutions

Letter of Credit

Consider a Transferable Letter of Credit to facilitate two-way trade transactions while you save time and resources.

Back-to-Back Letter of Credit

You can secure the goods with your buyer's letter of credit as collateral. Issue a letter of credit and secure the goods. Your buyers' and sellers' identities are not revealed.

Credit Protection

Leverage our market-leading expertise and broad network of partners to secure your trade receivables so that you can trade with confidence.

Import Loan

Trade with confidence and benefit from the experience and support of our specialists.

Export Loan

Improve your cash flow with export discounting of your invoices.

Sellers

As a seller, your main concerns are:

  • Secure your deal
  • Export solutions
  • Manage your cash flow



Our solutions

Export Letter of Credit

Export your goods with peace of mind by securing payment from your buyer subject that the documents comply with UCP 600 and letter of credit.

Export Documentary Collection

Proceed with the shipment of your goods while ensuring your export document reaches your buyer upon payment or commitment to future payment.

Bank Guarantee / Standby Letters of Credit

Secure your deal with your buyer by leveraging MCBs reputation and assurance of payment on your behalf upon the presentation of complying documents to the buyer.

Credit Protection

Leverage our market-leading expertise and broad network of partners to secure your trade receivables so that you can trade with confidence.

Export Loan

Improve your cash flow with export discounting of your invoices.
